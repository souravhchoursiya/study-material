# 🎓 [Universal Study Portal](https://souravhchoursiya.github.io/study-material/)

Welcome to the Universal Study Portal! This platform provides secure, organized access to your course materials.

## 🌟 How it Works

The portal is designed for ease of use, with a simple two-step process to get you started and keep your study materials organized by subject.

### Step 1: Registration
Before accessing materials, you must register your details.
* **Go to the "Register Here" tab.**
* Enter your **Full Name** and **Form Number**.
* Click "Complete Registration".
* *Note: Your Form Number is your unique identifier. Make sure it is entered correctly.*

### Step 2: Accessing Notes
Once registered, you can unlock folders for any available subject.
* **Go to the "Unlock Notes" tab.**
* Select the **Subject** you wish to study from the dropdown menu.
* Enter your registered **Form Number**.
* Click "Access Material".
* You will instantly see a list of all available files for that subject. Click "Open" to view or download a file.

## 🔒 Security & Privacy

* **Form Number Access:** Your Form Number acts as your password. Do not share it with others.
* **Secure Environment:** This portal operates on a secure, serverless architecture. Your registration details and access logs are stored safely and are not accessible to other students.
* **Dynamic Updates:** When new subjects or files are added by the department, they will automatically appear in your dropdown menu without you needing to do anything.

## 🛠️ Need Help?

If you experience any issues (e.g., your Form Number isn't working, or you are having trouble downloading a file), please use the **"Need Help? Contact CR"** link at the bottom of the portal. 

Clicking the link will automatically open WhatsApp with a pre-filled message directed to your Class Representative.
